About Me

I have lived all over the US from California to Georgia, and moved to Colorado from New York. My husband and I have lived in Denver for 12 years. We have two elementary-aged children, 2 rescue-dogs, a rescue-cat, fish, and bees -- We keep pretty busy. I have served on several community boards and enjoy volunteer work. I love spending time outside playing sports, gardening, and hiking. With technology and remote work becoming more prevalent, I decided, during the pandemic, expanding my graphic design skills into more full stack development would be opportune. There are many industries taking advantage of going online and I would like to be apart of the expansion.


EDUCATION: I earned an MBA from the University of Denver. Prior to that I earned an MFA and BFA in Graphic Design from the Savannah College of Design.>
